Figure 1: Tape Collected miRNA Reproducibility and Stability

  • A) Spearman correlation for miRNA from serial tape sampling of single nevi on different individuals.
  • B) Read length distribution: FFPE (pink) vs. tape (blue).
  • C) Sequencing composition: tape has higher miRNA reads and fewer adapter dimers than FFPE.

Comments

  • Reproducibility is typically based on the [Bland-Altman plot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bland%E2%80%93Altman_plot)
  • This is also related to RLE plots frequently used in RNA-Seq
  • These measures of reproducibility or sample similarity often produce some of the most useful sample quality metrics and can be used for inclusion/exclusion criteria
  • Pearson CC computed on the un-logged data is not a valid similarity.
  • Pearson CC is risky to interpret without showing the scatter.
